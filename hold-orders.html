What are Hold Orders?

Hold orders are restrictions placed on your account that may prevent you from:

  • Registering for classes
  • Receiving transcripts
  • Graduating
  • Accessing certain university services

Common Types of Holds

Hold Type Description How to Resolve
Financial Hold Outstanding tuition or fees Make payment through the Payments page
Library Hold Overdue books or unpaid fines Return books or pay fines at the library
Academic Hold Academic probation or missing requirements Contact your academic advisor
Administrative Hold Missing documents or information Contact the Registrar's Office
